This was a geology major course covering sedimentology, including the origin and transport of sedimentary materials, physical properties and classification of sedimentary rocks, depositional environments (continental, marginal-marine, and marine), and stratigraphy and basin analysis. As a TA, I was responsible for teaching field trip description and microscope observations, as well as composing exam questions.
