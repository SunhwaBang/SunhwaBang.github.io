I am a researcher with a broad interest in paleoclimatology and paleoenvironmental reconstruction. My research focuses on isotope geochemistry of sediments and sedimentary rocks, and clay mineral analysis, which I apply as proxies to understand how the ocean and atmosphere have evolved over geological timescales. I am also continuously developing analytical techniques using MC-ICP-MS, LA-ICP-MS and AI-image detecting to refine these reconstructions.

Currently, I am working as a postdoctoral researcher at the Research Institute of Molecular Alchemy, Gyeongsang National University.
